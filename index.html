About Us & Our Goals

Our Mission

Pawsitive Impact is dedicated to securing justice for animals and promoting comprehensive animal welfare. We believe every animal deserves respectful treatment and proper care, and work tirelessly to make this vision a reality through community engagement and education.

Our research revealed that countless dogs and cats suffer on the streets due to neglect and abandonment. Organizations like Hong Kong Dog Rescue have been making a remarkable difference by providing safe havens for these animals until they find forever homes.

Inspired by their success, we've collaborated with them to amplify our impact through volunteer work, fundraising campaigns, and educational initiatives via social media and community events.

Our Vision

"To create an informed and compassionate community where every donation, every volunteer hour, and every shared story contributes to lasting improvements in animal welfare."

Support

We will provide assistance to HKDR through donations and volunteer hours.

Care

We will assist HKDR to ensure that rescued animals receive the medical attention, nutrition, and love they need to thrive.

Advocacy

We will advocate for stronger animal protection laws and educate students about responsible pet ownership.
